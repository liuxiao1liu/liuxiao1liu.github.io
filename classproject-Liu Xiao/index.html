School Profiles

Montgomery Auburn University is an urban campus, elegant and quiet, is a good place to study.
Auburn University of Montgomery, consisting of five schools of business, education, liberal Arts, nursing and Science, has obtained national and international certifications in business, public administration, education, Medical Technology, law and nursing, and has the right to confer bachelor's, master's and Doctor's degrees.

  • Scientific research and social services have been coordinated and yielded fruitful results.
  • AUM adheres to the concept of open schooling and carries out international exchanges and cooperation.
  • AUM has a complete set of disciplines and a reasonable structure, and has basically established a comprehensive and research-oriented university discipline system.
